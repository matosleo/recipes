Ingredients:
 - 10 ounces hot black coffee (freshly brewed)
 - 1/4 teaspoon chocolate extract (or to haste)
 - 1/4 teaspoon hazelnut extract (or to haste)
 - 1/4 teaspoon rum extract (or to haste)
 - Splenda sugar substitute

 Diractions:
 1. Pour fresh coffee into mug
 2. Add the extracts and Splenda.
 3. Stir and enjoy!
